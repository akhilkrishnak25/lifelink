Precautions Before Donating Blood

These are general guidelines. Always follow the advice of medical professionals.

âœ… Before Donation
  • Sleep well (at least 7-8 hours) the night before.
  • Eat a healthy meal 2-3 hours before donating.
  • Drink plenty of water (stay hydrated).
  • Avoid alcohol for 24 hours before donation.
  • Avoid fatty foods (can affect blood tests).
  • Carry a valid ID proof.
â›” Who Should Not Donate (Temporary)
  • If you have fever, cold, flu, or any infection.
  • If you had a tattoo/piercing in the last 6 months (varies by region).
  • If you had major surgery recently.
  • If you are taking antibiotics (ask doctor).
ğŸ©¹ After Donation
  • Keep the bandage on for at least 2-4 hours.
  • Drink extra fluids for the next 24 hours.
  • Avoid heavy exercise and lifting for 24 hours.
  • If you feel dizzy, sit/lie down, and inform staff.
