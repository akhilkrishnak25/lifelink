How It Works

For Donors:

  • Register as a donor with your blood group and location
  • Toggle your availability status anytime
  • View nearby emergency blood requests
  • Accept requests and help save lives

For Receivers:

  • Create emergency blood requests with hospital details
  • Specify blood group, urgency, and location
  • View interested donors and their contact information
  • Connect with donors and coordinate donation
